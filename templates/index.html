WHO ARE WE?

The PID Team is a group of young engineers hailing from Almaty, Kazakhstan. With 12 members on board, we've united our efforts to bring innovative projects to life. Last season, three of our high school seniors competed as part of the QStem team in the FTC category, while four younger students engaged in the FLL category. This year, we merged forces to establish the PID team, where we combine our expertise and passion for technical challenges. Our collective is a space where experience meets ambition, where high schoolers and younger students collaborate on new challenges and ideas, striving for technological innovation and creative growth.
